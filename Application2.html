The Ethereal

The Ethereal is a concept of a Luxury Restaurant which also offers Meeting Rooms and this is the website I designed for it.

The main target audience of this restaurant are the plethora of business people in The Hague who are looking for an elegant, classic and luxurious place to hold their business meetings while also dining on exquisitely prepared meals. You could also wish the food to be delivered to your office.
