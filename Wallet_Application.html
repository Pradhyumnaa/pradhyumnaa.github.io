The Problem

The Average American owns around 4 Credit Cards and managing multiple bank accounts from their respective application or website can get tedious.

My Solution

Magnetar is an app that helps to organize several bank accounts. Track your recent transactions and create a budget for each of your expenses by creating "virtual" spaces within your own account.

Branding

The next step was to decide on a brand identity. I wanted to create a platform that the users can trust, especially when dealing with money.



How it works

I mocked up and prototyped a Mobile App in order to prove the concept
